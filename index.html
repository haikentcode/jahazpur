About Jahazpur
Jahazpur is a City in Jahazpur Tehsil in Bhilwara District of Rajasthan State, India. It belongs to Ajmer Division . It is located 83 KM towards East from District head quarters Bhilwara. It is a Tehsil head quarter. Jahazpur Pin code is 311201 and postal head office is Jahazpur
